What is a Website

Definition, types, and core uses.

All the ways you can use websites

  • Personal portfolio — showcase work, attract clients.
  • Business site — sales, lead capture, booking.
  • E‑commerce — sell products, subscriptions.
  • Landing pages — marketing funnels and ads.
  • Dashboards — internal tools and analytics.

How to implement in your life

  • Start small — build a portfolio and list services.
  • Use templates to speed delivery, then customize.
  • Automate hosting and deployment with providers like Netlify, Vercel, or simple FTP.

How to make money

  • Freelance projects — charge per site or per feature.
    • - **Suggested pricing** — beginner: $500–$2,500; intermediate: $3,000–$10,000; advanced: $15,000+.
  • Maintenance — monthly retainers for updates and hosting.
  • Templates & themes — sell prebuilt themes or components.

Who to sell to and how

  • Local small businesses — restaurants, salons, contractors.
  • Freelancers & creatives — portfolios and booking.
  • Agencies — subcontract parts of larger projects.
